CJB Summit Press Assets
=======================

Place the following files in this directory:

1. headshot.jpg - Professional headshot of Christopher J. Bradley
2. sunset-1.jpg - Sunset photo from the sailboat years
3. sunset-2.jpg - Another sunset photo from the sailboat years
4. logo.png - CJB Summit logo or promotional graphic

Also place in the parent /assets/ directory:
- CJB_Summit_Press_Kit.pdf - The official press kit PDF

To generate the PDF press kit:
1. Open /assets/press-kit.html in a browser
2. Press Ctrl+P (or Cmd+P on Mac)
3. Select "Save as PDF" as the destination
4. Use Letter paper size with default margins
5. Save as "CJB_Summit_Press_Kit.pdf" in the /assets/ directory
